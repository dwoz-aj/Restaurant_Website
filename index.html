Chicken Biryani

Ingrediants

  • 2 cups of Basmati rice
  • 2-3 tablespoons of vegetable oil or ghee (clarified butter)
  • 500 grams of bone-in chicken pieces
  • 1 teaspoon of red chili powder,turmeric powder and garam masala.

Procedure

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice. Set aside.
  2. Soak Basmati rice and cook with spices until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, sauté sliced onions until golden brown.Add green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, and chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until it changes color.Layer partially cooked rice over the chicken.Sprinkle saffron milk, fried onions, and chopped herbs.Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.Drizzle ghee and serve hot.

Rasgulla

Ingrediants

  • Chhena (paneer)
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom
  • Rose water (optional)

Procedure

  1. Prepare Sugar Syrup.Make Chenna (Indian Cottage Cheese).
  2. Knead Chenna.Shape into Balls.
  3. Boil in Sugar Syrup.Cook & Expand.
  4. Cool & Serve.
